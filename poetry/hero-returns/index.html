Hero returns!

March 21, 2021 Â· One minute read

Trumpets beat

roses and jasmines

shower

from above

incense burns, hiding

the smell

of blood underneath

the white shroud

and trumpets drown

wails of

the son, the daughter

the mother, the father

and the widow

all the countrymen hail

the death of their

martyr.
